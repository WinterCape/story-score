# StoryScore -- Store Listing Materials

## App Store (iOS)

### Short Description (30 chars max)
Score tracker for card games

### Full Description
StoryScore is a score companion for storytelling card games. Track every round, see who fooled whom, and celebrate the winner with style.

Set up your game in seconds. Add player names, pick colors, and start scoring. StoryScore handles the math so you can focus on the stories.

Features:
- Automatic scoring with full rule support
- Round-by-round history with vote breakdowns
- Live leaderboard that updates after every round
- Undo and edit any round
- Export and share game results
- Works completely offline -- no account, no internet required
- Beautiful dark and light themes

Whether you play with 3 friends or a full table, StoryScore keeps the game moving.

### iOS (App Store Review)

```
StoryScore is a score tracking companion for storytelling card games
(the genre where one player gives a clue and others vote on which
card matches the clue).

To test the app:
1. Tap "New Game" on the home screen.
2. Add 3-6 player names and tap "Start Game".
3. On the Round screen, select the storyteller, then tap each
   voter's choice.
4. Tap "Submit Round" to see scores update on the Scoreboard tab.
5. Repeat for additional rounds, or tap "End Game" to see final
   standings.

No login is required. No data is collected. The app works fully
offline.

feature and additional themes. The core game experience is fully
functional without any purchase.
```

### StoryScore Support

**What is StoryScore?**
StoryScore is a score tracking companion for storytelling card games. It automates scoring, tracks round history, and shows a live leaderboard.

**How do I start a game?**
Tap "New Game," add player names (3-10 players), pick colors, and tap "Start Game."

**How does scoring work?**
StoryScore follows the standard scoring rules for storytelling card games. After each round, select the storyteller, record each player's vote, and the app calculates points automatically.

**Can I undo a round?**
Yes. On the History tab, you can undo the last round or edit any previous round.

**Is my data private?**
Yes. All data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to any server. See our Privacy Policy for details.

**Do I need an internet connection?**
No. StoryScore works completely offline.

**How do I export a game?**
On the Scoreboard or Endgame screen, tap the share/export button to save or send your game data.
