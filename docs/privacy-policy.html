StoryScore

Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 18, 2026

StoryScore is built as a fully offline, local-only application. This policy explains how the app handles your data.

Data Collection

StoryScore does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. No analytics, no crash reporting, no usage tracking.

Accounts

StoryScore does not require or support user accounts. There is no sign-up, login, or authentication.

Network Access

StoryScore does not require an internet connection to function. The only network activity, if any, is initiated by the operating system for in-app purchase verification (if you choose to make a purchase via the App Store or Google Play).

Local Storage

All game data (player names, scores, round history) is stored locally on your device using an on-device SQLite database. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export and share it.

Third-Party Services

StoryScore uses RevenueCat for in-app purchase management and receipt validation. RevenueCat processes purchase data through Google Play or Apple App Store billing. RevenueCat assigns an anonymous identifier to your device — no personal information (name, email, etc.) is collected or shared. For details, see RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

No other third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services are used.

Children's Privacy

StoryScore does not collect any data from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.

Contact

For questions about this policy, visit our support page.
