What is StoryScore?

StoryScore is a score tracking companion for storytelling card games. It automates scoring, tracks round history, and shows a live leaderboard.

How do I start a game?

Tap "New Game," add player names (3-10 players), pick colors, and tap "Start Game."

How does scoring work?

StoryScore follows the standard scoring rules for storytelling card games. After each round, select the storyteller, record each player's vote, and the app calculates points automatically.

Can I undo a round?

Yes. On the History tab, you can undo the last round or edit any previous round.

Is my data private?

Yes. All data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to any server. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Do I need an internet connection?

No. StoryScore works completely offline.

How do I export a game?

On the Scoreboard or Endgame screen, tap the share/export button to save or send your game data.
